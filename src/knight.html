THE KNIGHT

The Knight is a small yet formidable warrior, clad in a simple horned shell and wielding a nail as a weapon. Its minimalistic design emphasizes mystery and elegance, allowing it to embody both resilience and quiet determination in the vast, forgotten kingdom of Hallownest.
